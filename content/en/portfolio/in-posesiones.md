IN-Possessions is a series of video-based urban actions in which the Black body dialogues with and disrupts emblematic sites of the city, spaces charged with racist symbols and colonial memories.

Through movement and ritual presence, the body becomes possessed by multiple memories while simultaneously taking possession of the territory. In this passage, the Black body ceases to be a fixed identity and becomes plural: many bodies, many presences, many ways of inhabiting the city through revelation and rebellion.

**In the city**  
Spirits take possession of a Black body  
The possessed Black body begins to take possession of the city  
The Black body ceases to be a body  
The Black body ceases to be Black  
.

.

A Black body…  
—  
It transcends form, time, and space

And moves inhabiting many meanings,  
And moves to not remain silent,  
And moves in search of a revelation; a rebellion

The Black body ceases to be a body  
The Black body ceases to be Black

Now it is  
…Many Black bodies
