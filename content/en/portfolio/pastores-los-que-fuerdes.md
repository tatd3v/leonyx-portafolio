Performance directed by Alejandro Penagos and David Felipe Escobar that dreamily opens the exhibition *Aunque es de noche* by David Felipe Escobar.

The work portrays homoerotic desire as a possible paradise, fusing painting and performance in a ceremonial opening act.

**Direction:** Alejandro Penagos & David Felipe Escobar  
**Collaboration:** Le'Onyx Da'Vida  
**Venue:** Galería Sketch, Bogotá
